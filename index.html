Biography

I am a 3rd-year Ph.D. candidate of Dept. Electronic Engineering and Information Science (EEIS) in University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) and a member of Intelligent Media Computing Lab.

Before that, I received my B.Sc. degree in Communication Engineering in Jun. 2015. From Feb. 2017 to May. 2017, I was a research intern in National Institute of Informatics (NII) , Tokyo, Japan.

Research Interests

My research interests include some sub-fields of Computer Vision and Machine Learning:

  • Image/Video Quality Assessment (3D-QA) is significant for quality monitoring and optimization of the existing 3D image and video services.

  • Visual Depth Perception (VDP) is an important dimension to estimate depth from the perceived 2D images in 3D vision.

  • Deep Convolutional Neural Network (DCNN) is a type of artificial neural network and widely used in image and video related research works.
