Wei ZHOU

Assistant Professor at Cardiff University

I am currently an Assistant Professor in the School of Computer Science and Informatics at Cardiff University, United Kingdom. I am a committee member of British Standards Institution (BSI). I am also a member of Visual Computing Research Group, which is led by Prof. Paul Rosin. I am participating in the JPEG standardization activities. My research interests mainly focus on perceptual image and video processing.

About Me

  • Jul. 2023 - Present, I am an Assistant Professor (UK Lecturer) in the School of Computer Science and Informatics at Cardiff University, United Kingdom.

  • Aug. 2021 - Jun. 2023, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Dept. of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) at University of Waterloo, supervised by Prof. Zhou Wang (IEEE Fellow, Royal Society of Canada Fellow, Canadian Academy of Engineering Fellow, Canada Research Chair).

  • Dec. 2019 - Aug. 2021, I was a joint Ph.D. in Dept. of ECE at UWaterloo, supervised by Prof. Zhou Wang (IEEE Fellow, Royal Society of Canada Fellow, Canadian Academy of Engineering Fellow, Canada Research Chair), and won Outstanding Doctoral Thesis Award in 2022.

  • Sept. 2017 - Jun. 2021, I was a Ph.D. Candidate in MOE-Microsoft Key Laboratory of Multimedia Computing and Communication at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), supervised by Prof. Weiping Li (IEEE Fellow) and Prof. Zhibo Chen.

  • Oct. 2019 - Dec. 2019, I was an Intern of Alibaba Innovative Research, Video Cloud, Alibaba Cloud, mentored by Bin Li and Maolin Liu, with Outstanding Award.

  • Jun. 2018 - Nov. 2018, I was a Research Intern in Intelligent Media Group, Microsoft Research, mentored by Wenjun Zeng (IEEE Fellow) and Yuwang Wang, with the Award of Excellence.

  • Sept. 2016 - Aug. 2018, I was a Research Assistant in Intel ICRI MNC, supervised by Weiping Li (IEEE Fellow) and Zhibo Chen, joint with Tsinghua University and Southeast University.

  • Feb. 2017 - May 2017, I was a Visiting Scholar in National Institute of Informatics (NII), Tokyo, Japan, mentored by Yi Yu.

  • Sept. 2015 - Aug. 2017, I was a Master Student in MOE-Microsoft Key Laboratory of Multimedia Computing and Communication at USTC, supervised by Weiping Li (IEEE Fellow) and Zhibo Chen. Before that, I received my B.Sc. degree in Communication Engineering in 2015 (minor in Business), and won Outstanding Bachelor Thesis Award in 2016.

    • LION Lab

    I direct the InteLligent multImedia perceptiOn and generatioN (LION) research lab. My lab focuses on studying different perceptual and generative models in the intelligent multimedia field. Our lab is always looking for talented and self-motivated students.

    Each year, our School has a number of funding schemes to support PhD research (School Studentships). Chinese PhD applicants are also welcome to apply via Cardiff University-China Scholarship Council joint-funded PhD Scholarship. If you are interested in working with me on cutting-edge image processing/multimedia research, please drop me an email.

    Cardiff University is ranked top 5 (CSRankings) across the UK in the areas of Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, Computer Graphics, and Natural Language Processing. I have worked with more than 15 students and extensively collaborated with researchers in both academia and industry worldwide. I will actively recommend my students for internships (Google, Meta, Intel, Microsoft, Tencent, Alibaba, etc.) and help them establish networking.
