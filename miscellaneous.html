Personal Bio

I was born about one year before 1995 (likely to be a post-95 generation but not), in Nanjing, Jiangsu, P. R. China. When I was in preschool, I skipped a grade to a primary school in Nanjing. At 17 years old, I became an undergraduate student and then a postdoctoral fellow in Canada (27 years old). I got faculty job interviews and offers from US, UK, and Singapore at 28 years old, and I became a doctoral supervisor at 29 years old. My parents and grandparents are both engaged in government and education careers, and so am I. It is amazing. I have a wide range of interests, including photography, guitar, table tennis, basketball, chess, etc. I also love traveling.
