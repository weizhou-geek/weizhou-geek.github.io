Biography

I have been an Assistant Professor (UK Lecturer) in the School of Computer Science and Informatics at Cardiff University, United Kingdom since 2023. I am a member of Visual Computing Research Section, which is led by Prof. Paul Rosin. I am also a committee member of British Standards Institution and EPSRC Peer Review College. I serve as General Chair for the 1st Cardiff Image & Vision Computing Workshop and Chair for the Elections Committee of IEEE UK & Ireland SPS Chapter. I am a Senior Member of IEEE (2024, previously Member 2021, Student Member 2019). I was named in the 2024 edition of the Stanford University list of World Top 2% Scientists.

I was born in Nanjing, Jiangsu, China. Before moving to UK, I studied and worked in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of Waterloo, Canada under the supervision of Prof. Zhou Wang (FIEEE, FRSC, FCAE, Canada Research Chair) from 2019 to 2023. During the period, I received my PhD degree in 2021, with outstanding thesis award. I used to be a visiting professor at Dalian University of Technology (2024), a visiting scholar at Japan National Institute of Informatics (2017), a research assistant with Intel (2016-2018), a research intern in Microsoft Research (2018, with excellent award) and Alibaba Cloud (2019, with outstanding award). I had an educational background in Electrical Engineering at University of Science and Technology of China, under the supervision of Prof. Zhibo Chen and Prof. Weiping Li (Life Fellow of IEEE, Dean, Served as the Editor-in-Chief for IEEE TCSVT).

My research interests mainly focus on image/video processing, multimedia computing, applied perception, human-centric AI, computational photography, imaging, vision, displays, and machine learning. I aim to develop efficient and intelligent visual processing algorithms based on human perception, helping improve the quality of experience for human visual systems. I received IEEE CASS VSPC Rising Star Honorable Mention Award (the first recipient in IQA research field worldwide) in 2024, ACM SIGMM Outstanding Doctoral Dissertation Award (China Section, 1st nationwide) in 2022, and Grand Challenge Winner Award (1st place) in CVPR CLIC 2021.

I am actively looking for self-motivated students (PhD, MPhil, and visiting scholars). Please reach out to me if interested.
We are organizing the 1st Cardiff Image and Vision Computing Workshop.

LION Lab

I direct the InteLligent multImedia perceptiOn and generatioN (LION) research lab. My lab focuses on studying different perceptual and generative models in the intelligent multimedia field. Our lab is always looking for talented and self-motivated students.

Each year, our School has a number of funding schemes to support PhD research (School Studentships). Chinese PhD applicants are also welcome to apply via Cardiff University-China Scholarship Council joint-funded PhD Scholarship. If you are interested in working with me on cutting-edge image processing/multimedia research, please drop me an email.

Cardiff University (top 1 university in Wales, part of the Russell Group) is ranked top 2 (CSRankings) across the UK in the areas of Computer Vision, Computer Graphics, and Natural Language Processing. I have worked with 30 students and extensively collaborated with researchers in both academia and industry worldwide. I will actively recommend my students for internships (Google, Meta, Intel, Microsoft, Tencent, Alibaba, etc.) and help them establish networking.
